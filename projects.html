My Recent Work

THE POWER OF THOUGHTFUL ONBOARDING

I worked as a UX/UI designer at the startup Ronday Technologies on their online 3D conferencing and hangout platform. As to be expected of startup work, I performed a myriad of tasks both design-related and otherwise.

MAKING WEB ACCESSIBILITY, ACCESSIBLE

I volunteered with a nonprofit to investigate the accessibility status of their website.This work involved a 7-page report to help bring the website to AAA compliance. Following the report, I created a free online tool for anyone looking to review their website for accessibility reasons.

ANYTIME ROBOT NECK MASSAGES

For my master’s thesis, I completed a first-person Somaesthetic design project around the sensation of “rocking”. The final result is a shape-changing pillow called the “Neck Hammock”.

BRINGING LIGHTHEARTED FUN TO DARKENED PLAYGROUNDS

To spruce up Swedish playgrounds during the winter, I designed, tested, and prototyped light-up tiles! This was a group project during my master's thesis which took 3 months and a budget of 1000SEK (~98 USD).
